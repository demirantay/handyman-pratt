ABOUT HANDYMAN PRATT

WE ARE SEATTLE'S LEADING HANDYMAN WORKERS

My name is Juchi Pratt, and I began my journey into the world of handyman services just over a year ago here in the vibrant Seattle area. Drawing inspiration from a family background in craftsmanship, I've always had a knack for fixing things and improving spaces. This passion took root early on, influenced by experiences helping out around the house and assisting neighbors with their projects.

Starting out, I quickly realized that my skills and dedication could benefit others beyond just family and friends. I decided to formalize my expertise and offer professional handyman services to homeowners and businesses alike. Every project, whether big or small, is approached with the same level of care and attention to detail that I learned from mentors and honed through hands-on experience.

Choosing this path wasn't just a career decision; it was a commitment to carrying forward a tradition of quality craftsmanship. My journey into the handyman trade has been a rewarding one, filled with learning opportunities and the satisfaction of seeing happy clients. I look forward to continuing to serve the Seattle community, bringing both skill and passion to every project entrusted to me.
